# Business Requirements Analysis

## Project Title

AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System (AIRCMS)

---

## Introduction

The AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System (AIRCMS) is a Salesforce-based application developed to automate and optimize the end-to-end recruitment process. It provides a centralized platform for recruiters, hiring managers, and HR teams to manage candidate information, job openings, interviews, approvals, and onboarding activities. The solution also leverages Agentforce AI to support intelligent hiring decisions.

---

## Existing Business Problems

Many organizations currently manage recruitment using spreadsheets, emails, and disconnected software tools. This creates several challenges:

- Duplicate candidate records
- Lost or misplaced applications
- Delayed interview scheduling
- Manual resume screening
- Inconsistent hiring decisions
- Lack of transparency
- Poor recruitment tracking
- High manual effort
- Difficulty generating recruitment reports

---

## Business Requirements

The organization requires a centralized CRM solution that can:

- Manage candidate profiles
- Manage job openings
- Track job applications
- Schedule interviews
- Manage offer approvals
- Provide AI-generated candidate summaries
- Generate candidate scores
- Provide hiring recommendations
- Automate recruiter follow-ups
- Generate reports and dashboards
- Maintain secure access to recruitment data

---

## Objectives

### Centralized Recruitment Management

The application should provide one platform to manage:

- Candidate Profiles
- Job Openings
- Applications
- Interview Stages
- Offer Approvals
- AI Hiring Recommendations

### Recruitment Automation

Automate:

- Candidate Screening
- Interview Scheduling
- Offer Approval
- Recruiter Notifications
- Status Updates

### AI-Driven Hiring

Provide:

- AI Candidate Score
- Resume Summary
- Hiring Recommendation
- Risk Analysis

### Security

Ensure:

- Role-Based Access
- Approval Process
- Secure Candidate Data

- Cloud-based CRM
- Flow Automation
- Agentforce AI
- Lightning Experience
- Approval Processes
- Reports & Dashboards
- Secure Role-Based Access
- Scalable Architecture
