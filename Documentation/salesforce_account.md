# AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System (AIRCMS)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System (AIRCMS)** is a Salesforce-based recruitment platform designed to automate and streamline the complete hiring lifecycle. The application enables recruiters, hiring managers, and HR teams to efficiently manage candidates, job openings, interviews, offer approvals, and onboarding through a centralized Salesforce platform.

By leveraging **Salesforce Administration**, **Flows**, **Approval Processes**, **Lightning Experience**, and **Agentforce AI**, the system minimizes manual work, standardizes hiring decisions, and provides AI-driven candidate evaluation and recommendations.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Centralize recruitment management within Salesforce.
- Automate recruitment workflows using Salesforce Flows.
- Improve hiring decisions with AI-powered candidate evaluation.
- Standardize approval processes for offers.
- Enhance recruiter productivity through automation.
- Ensure secure access using Salesforce security features.
- Generate reports and dashboards for recruitment analytics.
