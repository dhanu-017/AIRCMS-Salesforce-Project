# AI-Powered Recruitment System – Salesforce Email Templates

## Project Overview

This project implements automated email notifications for an AI-powered Recruitment Management System using Salesforce Classic Email Templates. The email templates improve communication between recruiters and hiring managers by automatically sending notifications for high-priority candidate applications, salary approval requests, and approval outcomes.

---

# Features

* High Priority Candidate Notification
* High Salary Offer Approval Notification
* Candidate Approval Outcome Notification
* Automated Recruitment Communication
* Salesforce Classic Email Templates
* AI-Driven Recruitment Workflow Support

---

# Technologies Used

* Salesforce CRM
* Classic Email Templates
* Merge Fields
* Email Alerts
* Salesforce Setup
* AI Recruitment Management System

---

# Activity 1: High Priority Candidate Application Notification Email Template

## Objective

Notify the recruiter when AI identifies a candidate application as high priority and requires immediate attention.

### Template Details

**Template Name**

High Priority Candidate Application Notification

**Subject**

High-Priority Candidate Application Assigned to You

### Email Content Includes

* Recruiter Name
* Candidate Name
* Job Opening
* Experience Level
* AI Candidate Score
* Application Date
* Instruction to contact the candidate within 24 hours

### Outcome

Recruiters receive immediate notifications for high-priority candidate applications, enabling faster recruitment decisions.

---

# Activity 2: High Salary Candidate Offer Approval Email Template

## Objective

Notify the Hiring Manager whenever a candidate offer with a high salary is submitted for approval.

### Template Details

**Template Name**

High Salary Candidate Offer Approval Notification

**Subject**

Candidate Offer Awaiting Salary Approval

### Email Content Includes

* Hiring Manager Name
* Candidate Name
* Job Opening
* Proposed Offer Amount
* AI Candidate Score
* Application Status
* Request to approve or reject the offer

### Outcome

Hiring Managers are automatically informed about high-value salary offers requiring approval.

---

# Activity 3: Candidate Application Approval Outcome Notification Email Template

## Objective

Notify the recruiter after the Hiring Manager completes the approval process.

### Template Details

**Template Name**

Candidate Application Approval Status Notification

**Subject**

Candidate Offer Approval Decision

### Email Content Includes

* Recruiter Name
* Candidate Name
* Job Opening
* Approval Status
* Final Offer Amount
* Instructions for the next recruitment steps

### Outcome

Recruiters receive timely updates on approval decisions, allowing them to continue the recruitment process efficiently.
