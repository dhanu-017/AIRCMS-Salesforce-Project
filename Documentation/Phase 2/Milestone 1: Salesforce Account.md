# Milestone 1: Salesforce Account

## Overview

This milestone focuses on creating and activating a Salesforce Developer Account, which serves as the foundation for building and testing Salesforce applications. The Developer Edition provides a free cloud environment where users can learn Salesforce, create custom applications, and practice using Salesforce features without affecting production systems.

---

# Objective

The objective of this milestone is to:

* Understand the basics of Salesforce.
* Create a Salesforce Developer Edition account.
* Activate the account through email verification.
* Access the Salesforce Setup page.
* Prepare the development environment for future project implementation.

---

# What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform that helps organizations manage customer relationships, sales, marketing, customer service, analytics, and business automation from a single platform.

It enables businesses to improve productivity through automation, secure cloud infrastructure, real-time reporting, and AI-powered capabilities.

---

# Milestone Activities

## Activity 1: Creating a Salesforce Developer Account

A free Salesforce Developer Edition account was created by completing the registration process.

### Registration Steps

1. Visit the Salesforce Developer signup page.
2. Enter the required information:

   * First Name
   * Last Name
   * Email Address
   * Job Title: **Developer**
   * Company: **College Name**
   * Country: **India**
3. Click **Sign Me Up** to submit the registration form.

---

## Activity 2: Account Activation

After registration, the account was activated through email verification.

### Activation Steps

1. Open the registered email inbox.
2. Locate the verification email from Salesforce.
3. Click **Verify Account**.
4. Create a secure password.
5. Select and answer a security question.
6. Click **Change Password**.
7. Successfully log in to the Salesforce Developer Organization.
8. Access the Salesforce Setup page.
