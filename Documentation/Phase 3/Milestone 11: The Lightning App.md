# Milestone 11 - Activity 1: Create a Lightning App

## Project
**AI-Powered Recruitment & Candidate Management System**

## Objective
Create a custom Lightning App that provides recruiters and hiring managers with quick access to recruitment-related objects, reports, and dashboards within Salesforce.

---

## Steps Performed

### Step 1: Open App Manager
- Navigate to **Setup**.
- Search for **App Manager** in the **Quick Find** box.
- Open **App Manager**.

### Step 2: Create a New Lightning App
- Click **New Lightning App**.
- Enter the following details:
  - **App Name:** AI-Powered Recruitment & Candidate Management System
- Upload an image related to recruitment or AI-powered hiring.
- Click **Next**.

### Step 3: Configure App Options
- Leave the default **App Options** unchanged.
- Click **Next**.

### Step 4: Utility Items
- Keep the default Utility Items.
- Click **Next**.

### Step 5: Configure Navigation Items
Move the following objects from **Available Items** to **Selected Items**:

- Candidate Applications
- Candidates (Contact)
- Job_Opening__c
- Accounts
- Reports
- Dashboards

Click **Next**.

### Step 6: Assign User Profiles
Assign the Lightning App to the following profiles:

- System Administrator
- Recruiter
- Hiring Manager

Move these profiles to **Selected Profiles**.

### Step 7: Save the App
- Click **Save & Finish**.

---

## Result
A custom Lightning App named **AI-Powered Recruitment & Candidate Management System** was successfully created and configured with recruitment-related navigation items. The app was assigned to the required user profiles, enabling recruiters, hiring managers, and administrators to efficiently manage candidates, job openings, reports, and dashboards from a centralized workspace.

---

## Outcome
- Created a custom Lightning App.
- Added recruitment-related navigation items.
- Uploaded a custom app image.
- Assigned the app to required user profiles.
- Improved accessibility and user experience for recruitment management within Salesforce.
